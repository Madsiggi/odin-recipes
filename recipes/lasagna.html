Lasagna

Description

This is a traditional italian recipe, composed of layers of square shaped pasta with different sauces in between them

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Grounded meat
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare some bolognese sauce with the tomatoes, meat and garlic
  2. First in a casserole, lay some sauce to cover the bottom surface
  3. Then add a layer of lasagna pasta that covers the sauce
  4. Repeat till filling the casserole
  5. Put in the oven for 20 minutes
  6. Cut in servings and serve in a round plate
